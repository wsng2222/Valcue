Valcue Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Valcue ("the App") handles information when you use the App. Valcue is published by Nogic.

1. Summary

  • Valcue does not require account creation or login.
  • Most workout, routine, weight, and settings data is stored locally on your device.
  • The App uses Google Mobile Ads, which may collect and process certain data such as device identifiers, IP address, diagnostics, and app interaction data.
  • The App does not sync your workout history or weight records to a developer-managed cloud server based on the current version of the App.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following data locally on your device using on-device storage:

  • Workout routines you create or edit
  • Workout session history such as date, duration, machine type, and routine information
  • Weight records and goal weight
  • App preferences such as language, units, theme, premium test state, sound settings, and workout reminder settings

This locally stored data is used to provide core app features and remains on your device unless you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall the App.

3. Information Collected by Third-Party SDKs

The App uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). According to Google's documentation, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share certain data, including:

  • Approximate location inferred from IP address
  • App interaction data
  • Diagnostic information
  • Device or other identifiers, such as advertising-related identifiers

This data may be used for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention, security, and compliance purposes.

Learn more: Google Mobile Ads data disclosure and Google Privacy Policy.

4. Notifications and Reminders

If you enable workout reminders, the App may request notification permission and schedule local notifications on your device. Reminder schedules and message preferences are stored locally on your device.

5. Sharing Features

The App may allow you to share workout results through your device's system share sheet. Any sharing you initiate is controlled by you and handled by the app or service you choose.

6. Data Retention

  • Locally stored app data remains on your device until you remove it, clear app data, or uninstall the App.
  • Data handled by third-party providers such as Google is retained according to those providers' own policies.

7. Data Deletion

Because the App does not currently provide account-based cloud storage, most app data can be removed by:

  • Deleting individual records inside the App where supported
  • Clearing the App's storage from your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App

Third-party data collected by advertising providers is controlled by those providers under their own privacy policies and user controls.

8. Children

Valcue is not intended for children and is designed for a general fitness audience, primarily adults.

9. Security

Based on the current App configuration, data transmitted off-device through integrated SDKs is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protections as described by the SDK provider.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If the policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, please contact the publisher through the support channel listed on the app's store page or via the project contact mechanism: https://github.com/wsng2222/Valcue/issues

If you later add account systems, cloud sync, analytics services, purchases, or additional SDKs, this policy should be updated before publishing that version.
